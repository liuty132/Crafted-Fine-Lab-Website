The renovation and regeneration of a historic courtyard compound. The original spatial logic of the hutong typology is preserved, while a lightweight steel roof structure is inserted to create a contemporary working environment within the historic fabric.

Light falls through the gap between old and new, inscribing the passage of time onto whitewashed walls. The threshold between eras is held open — neither erased nor romanticised — as an honest record of the building's continued life.
